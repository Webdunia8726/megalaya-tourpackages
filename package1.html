Guwahati & Kaziranga 4‑Days Trip

A journey of nature, spirituality & adventure

Overview

Experience Assam’s rich heritage and captivating wilderness with the Guwahati & Kaziranga 4‑Days Trip by NExplore NOW. Begin in Guwahati, visiting sacred Kamakhya Temple and enjoying a serene Brahmaputra River cruise. Then, venture to Kaziranga National Park for thrilling jeep and elephant safaris, spotting iconic one‑horned rhinos, deer, and exotic bird species. Surrounded by picturesque tea gardens and lush landscapes, this short yet enriching itinerary offers a perfect blend of spirituality, culture, and wildlife adventure — ideal for families, nature lovers, and friends seeking an unforgettable Northeast experience.

Itinerary

Morning: Arrive in Guwahati and meet your NExplore NOW representative. Begin your Assam adventure with a visit to the iconic Kamakhya Temple, one of the oldest and most revered Shakti Peethas in India.

Afternoon: Enjoy a traditional Assamese lunch and explore nearby markets for handicrafts and souvenirs.

Evening: Embark on a serene Brahmaputra River Cruise, enjoying picturesque sunset views and cultural performances on the boat.

Overnight Stay: Hotel in Guwahati.

Morning: After breakfast, drive to Kaziranga National Park, a World Heritage Site renowned for its one‑horned rhinos and rich biodiversity.

Afternoon: Check in to your resort and relax. Enjoy a traditional Assamese lunch.

Evening: Explore the nearby Tea Garden, experience tea tasting, and stroll through the serene plantations.

Overnight Stay: Stay in Kaziranga.

Early Morning: Begin with an Elephant Safari deep into the mist-clad grasslands of Kaziranga for a chance to spot the famous one‑horned rhinos, elephants, swamp deer, and a variety of birds.

Mid‑Morning: Return to the resort for breakfast and some rest.

Afternoon: Enjoy an exciting Jeep Safari through Kaziranga’s central range for closer encounters with wildlife and picturesque landscapes.

Evening: Experience a traditional Assamese Cultural Show featuring music, dance, and folklore.

Overnight Stay: Stay in Kaziranga.

Morning: Enjoy a delicious breakfast, and if time allows, visit a nearby village for a glimpse of rural Assamese life.

Late Morning: Begin the journey back to Guwahati.

Afternoon: Arrival in Guwahati. Transfer to the airport or railway station for your onward journey, carrying cherished memories of Assam’s wildlife, spirituality, and culture.
